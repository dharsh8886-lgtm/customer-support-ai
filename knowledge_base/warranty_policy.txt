Warranty Policy

Most products sold by TechNova Electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty.

The warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware failures under normal usage

The warranty does not cover:
- Physical damage
- Water damage
- Damage caused by accidents
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications

To claim warranty:
- Customers must provide the original purchase invoice.
- The product must be submitted to an authorized service center.
- Warranty verification may take 2–3 business days.
